Six schools, one campus

Find your discipline. Then cross it.

Northgate's deepest work happens at the seams — joint majors, cross-listed labs, and the kind of advising that makes you read outside your field.

School

Arts & Sciences

48 majors and 22 minors across the humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences. The largest school by enrollment.

School

Engineering & Computing

Twelve undergraduate majors and ten graduate programs, organized around six labs that span computing, energy, and bioengineering.

School

Business

Undergraduate concentration model and an MBA that admits 220 students each fall, with executive and online cohorts.

School

Education & Human Development

Teacher preparation, counseling psychology, and doctoral research in learning sciences. State certification built into every track.

School

Public Health

MPH, PhD, and dual degrees with Medicine and Law. Field placements across the region's hospital systems and community clinics.

School

Law

JD, LLM, and joint degrees. Clinic placements every semester from year one — appellate, immigration, civil rights, and family law.

Prospective undergrad

Apply by January 15.
