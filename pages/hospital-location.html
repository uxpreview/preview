Getting here

By car +

Take Lake Shore Drive to the East Ohio Street exit. Turn west to McClurg Court, then south to Erie Street. The main entrance is at the corner of Erie and McClurg.

Valet parking is available 6 AM – 10 PM at the main entrance for $18/day with appointment validation. Self-park in the McClurg garage at $14/day.

By CTA / public transit +

Red Line to Chicago station, then walk two blocks east on Chicago Avenue and one block south on Fairbanks. Step-free entrance at the main lobby.

Bus: Routes 66 (Chicago), 29 (State), and 124 (Navy Pier) all stop within two blocks. All buses are wheelchair-accessible.

By ride-share or taxi +

Ride-share drop-off at the main entrance on Erie Street. The covered driveway accommodates wheelchair vans. For accessible-vehicle pickup, request through your provider's preferred app or call our concierge desk at 312.238.4000.

Accessible entrance & navigation +

The main lobby entrance is step-free. Power doors at all primary entrances. Wheelchairs available at no cost at the lobby concierge desk. Tactile wayfinding and Braille floor signage throughout. Service animals welcome on every floor.
