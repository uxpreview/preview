Dr. Okonkwo is an attending physician on the Spinal Cord Injury service, where she leads acute rehabilitation for adults in the first ninety days after injury. Her practice combines daily bedside care with translational research on autonomic recovery.

She trained in physical medicine and rehabilitation at the University of Pennsylvania and completed a research fellowship at the National Institutes of Health. She joined Riverside Medical Center in 2014.

Recovery isn't linear. My job is to read the trajectory and to set goals that move with it — not against it.

Dr. Okonkwo
