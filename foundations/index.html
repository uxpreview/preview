Tier 1 · Foundations

The rules the whole system rests on.

Before any component: the principles that govern decisions, the tokens that hold every value, the layout primitives, and the evidence base that makes the system defensible in a client room.

Core

The bedrock: the rules that govern decisions, the values everything references, and the accessibility floor the whole system clears.

Principles

The governance rules every change follows — curate over add, content not style, honesty over polish.

Tokens

The only file with raw values: grayscale ramp, type and spacing scales, radii, borders, shadows, motion. Everything references these.

Accessibility

The WCAG 2.2 AA conformance map, the keyboard and interaction contracts, and the everyday practices every page follows.

Visual language

How the system looks and moves — type, icons, motion, and depth, all in grayscale.

Typography

One modular scale from a 12px eyebrow to an 88px display — hierarchy from size, weight, and space, never color.

Iconography

Monochrome line glyphs, inline as SVG in currentColor — one drawing convention, with accessible-labeling rules.

Motion

Three durations and two easings that clarify state changes, all collapsing to zero under reduced-motion.

Elevation & shadow

Four soft shadows and a three-plane layering model — borders separate, shadows elevate.

Structure & content

How a page is composed and written — the layout primitives and the words that fill them.

Layout

Container, stack, cluster, grid, sidebar — the structural primitives, plus the spacing scale and breakpoints, that compose every page.

Content & voice

Plain, direct, reassuring copy at a 6th–8th-grade reading level — voice, mechanics, and UI-copy rules.
